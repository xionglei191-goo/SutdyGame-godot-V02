六年级上册

《六年级上册》核心对话、重点句型及阅读语篇的所有英语句子，并进行逐句翻译：

### **Unit 1 What did you do during the holidays?**
*   Good morning, Mingming. Glad to see you again. (早上好，明明。很高兴再次见到你。)
*   Glad to see you too, Anne. What are you reading? (我也很高兴见到你，安妮。你在读什么？)
*   I'm reading an English storybook. I read many books during the holidays. (我正在读一本英语故事书。我在假期里读了很多书。)
*   What did you do during the holidays? (你在假期里做了什么？)
*   I learnt writing. I wrote a little storybook. (我学习了写作。我写了一本小故事书。)
*   Wow! That's good! Oh, great, Anne! Read it to us! (哇！那太好了！哦，太棒了，安妮！读给我们听听！)
*   Last week, Tim and Dino played an interesting game in class. (上周，蒂姆和迪诺在课上玩了一个有趣的游戏。)
*   Tim said, "Simon says, 'Stand up!'" Dino stood up. (蒂姆说：“西蒙说，‘起立！’”迪诺站了起来。)
*   Tim said, "Simon says, 'Sit down!'" Dino sat down. (蒂姆说：“西蒙说，‘坐下！’”迪诺坐下了。)
*   Tim said, "Take out your book." Dino did not take it out. (蒂姆说：“拿出你的书。”迪诺没有拿出来。)
*   The bell rang. Tim and Dino went out of the classroom. (铃响了。蒂姆和迪诺走出了教室。)
*   Dino said to Tim, "Simon says, 'Run to the tree and climb it.'" Tim ran to the tree and climbed it. (迪诺对蒂姆说：“西蒙说，‘跑到树那里爬上去。’”蒂姆跑到树那里爬了上去。)
*   And then Tim said to Dino, "Simon says, 'Run around the tree!'" But Dino didn't. (然后蒂姆对迪诺说：“西蒙说，‘绕着树跑！’”但是迪诺没有跑。)
*   "Why didn't you run around the tree?" asked Tim. Dino said, "Class is over!" (“你为什么不绕着树跑？”蒂姆问。迪诺说：“下课了！”)

### **Unit 2 Katie always gets up early**
*   Katie always gets up early. On weekdays, she always gets up at 6:30 a.m. (凯蒂总是起得很早。在工作日，她总是早上6:30起床。)
*   Her family often has breakfast at 6:45 a.m. (她的家人经常在早上6:45吃早饭。)
*   After breakfast, she waves goodbye to her mother and goes to school. (早饭后，她向妈妈挥手道别去上学。)
*   Katie returns home at 5:00 p.m. She often does her homework before dinner. She plays chess with her father after dinner. (凯蒂下午5:00回家。她经常在晚饭前做作业。晚饭后她和爸爸下国际象棋。)
*   John is an old scientist. He studies birds. He always gets up very early. (约翰是一位老科学家。他研究鸟类。他总是起得很早。)
*   He likes to listen to birds' singing. He often walks in the forests and finds new birds. (他喜欢听鸟儿唱歌。他经常在森林里散步并发现新的鸟类。)
*   Sometimes he takes photos of them. He can draw very well. (有时他给它们拍照。他画画得非常好。)
*   He often draws nice pictures of birds, old and young, big and small. He never hurts them. (他经常画好看的鸟类图片，老的和幼的，大的和小的。他从不伤害它们。)
*   Yesterday, he saw a few beautiful birds. They sang very well. He could not name them. (昨天，他看到了几只美丽的鸟。它们唱得很好。他叫不出它们的名字。)
*   Now he is waiting for them to come again. He is going to observe them and take photos of them. (现在他正等着它们再来。他打算观察它们并给它们拍照。)

### **Unit 3 I like my computer**
*   Dad, thank you. I like my computer. It's very fast. It will help you a lot. (爸爸，谢谢你。我喜欢我的电脑。它非常快。它会对你有很大帮助。)
*   Great! I saw an interesting bird in a book yesterday. Now I can search for a lot of things about it. (太棒了！昨天我在书里看到了一只有趣的鸟。现在我可以搜索很多关于它的信息。)
*   Good! You can also find out about countries in the world. OK! Let's try it now! (好！你也可以了解世界上的国家。好的！我们现在就试试吧！)
*   Dongdong was very excited. His father bought him a computer. It was his birthday present. (冬冬非常兴奋。他爸爸给他买了一台电脑。这是他的生日礼物。)
*   He could email his friends in Australia. He could do his homework on the computer, too. (他可以给他在澳大利亚的朋友发电子邮件。他也可以在电脑上做作业。)
*   Dongdong wanted to learn drawing and painting on the computer. Peter taught him. (冬冬想在电脑上学习画画和涂色。彼得教了他。)
*   Then Peter showed him how to search for a lot of things on the computer. (然后彼得向他展示了如何在电脑上搜索很多东西。)
*   He also showed Dongdong some computer games. He told Dongdong, "Don't play computer games too often. It is not good for your eyes." (他还向冬冬展示了一些电脑游戏。他告诉冬冬：“不要太频繁地玩电脑游戏。这对你的眼睛不好。”)

### **Unit 4 The Mid-Autumn Festival is coming**
*   The Mid-Autumn Festival is coming. Let's go and have a look. (中秋节要到了。我们去看看吧。)
*   Mum! There are many mooncakes in the shopping centre. (妈妈！购物中心里有很多月饼。)
*   Which type do you like, Binbin? I like the ones with lotus seeds. (你喜欢哪种，彬彬？我喜欢有莲子的那种。)
*   How much is a box of mooncakes? It's 50 yuan. (一盒月饼多少钱？50元。)
*   I'd like a box of lotus seed mooncakes and a box of red bean mooncakes, please. (请给我一盒莲蓉月饼和一盒红豆月饼。)
*   OK! Here you are. Thank you. (好的！给你。谢谢。)
*   I enjoy eating mooncakes. Hmm. This mooncake tastes good. (我喜欢吃月饼。嗯。这个月饼味道很好。)
*   It was the Mid-Autumn Festival yesterday. My grandfather got a gift from his friend. (昨天是中秋节。我爷爷收到了他朋友的一份礼物。)
*   After dinner, he put the box on the table and said to us, "Let's open the gift together." (晚饭后，他把盒子放在桌子上，对我们说：“我们一起打开礼物吧。”)
*   He opened the box. We saw a smaller box in it. He opened the smaller box. There was an even smaller box in that. (他打开了盒子。我们看到里面有一个更小的盒子。他打开了那个更小的盒子。里面还有一个更小的盒子。)
*   My grandfather opened this box and there was a big round mooncake. The words on the mooncake were, "Today's moon is round and bright." (我爷爷打开了这个盒子，里面有一个又大又圆的月饼。月饼上的字是：“今日的月亮又圆又亮”。)
*   Grandmother cut the mooncake. We all enjoyed eating the delicious mooncake. (奶奶切开了月饼。我们都喜欢吃这个美味的月饼。)

### **Unit 5 It will be sunny and cool tomorrow**
*   This is CCTV. It's time for the weather forecast. (这是中央电视台。天气预报时间到了。)
*   Tomorrow will be clear in Beijing. It will be sunny and cool. (明天北京将是晴天。天气会晴朗凉爽。)
*   It will be clear in Shanghai. There will be a light wind. (上海将是晴天。会有微风。)
*   There will be a strong wind in Xi'an. (西安将有大风。)
*   It will rain tomorrow in Changsha. But it won't be heavy. (长沙明天会下雨。但不会很大。)
*   It will be sunny and warm in Sanya. (三亚将是晴朗温暖的。)
*   Next Monday, there will be a big storm in Guangzhou. There will be a strong wind and heavy rain tomorrow. (下周一，广州将有一场大风暴。明天将有大风和大雨。)
*   No students will go to school. They will stay at home. People will park their cars in safe places. (没有学生会去上学。他们将呆在家里。人们会把车停在安全的地方。)
*   Tomorrow is Saturday. It will be sunny and cool in Beijing. People will go to the park. (明天是星期六。北京天气将晴朗凉爽。人们会去公园。)
*   Some children like to go to the zoo. Families will have fun. (一些孩子喜欢去动物园。家庭会玩得很开心。)

### **Unit 6 I will bring a big bottle of orange juice**
*   Autumn is coming. The weather is getting cool and nice. (秋天要来了。天气变得凉爽宜人。)
*   Tomorrow is Saturday. We can have a picnic in the park. (明天是星期六。我们可以在公园野餐。)
*   What are we going to bring for the picnic? (我们野餐要带些什么？)
*   I will bring some fruit. I will bring a few bars of chocolate. (我会带一些水果。我会带几块巧克力。)
*   I will bring a big bottle of orange juice. I will bring some peanuts and candies. (我会带一大瓶橙汁。我会带一些花生和糖果。)
*   Let's meet at the park at 9:00 a.m. Oh, I can't wait for tomorrow to come. (让我们上午9:00在公园见。哦，我等不及明天到来了。)
*   One sunny morning, Lingling, Dongdong, Peter and Anne went for a picnic at the beach. They played beach volleyball together. (一个阳光明媚的早晨，玲玲、冬冬、彼得和安妮去海滩野餐。他们一起打了沙滩排球。)
*   It was 12 o'clock. Anne and Lingling were tired. Dongdong and Peter were hungry. (到了12点。安妮和玲玲累了。冬冬和彼得饿了。)
*   They asked the girls, "Can we have lunch?" "OK!" the girls said. So they sat down to enjoy their lunch. (他们问女孩们：“我们可以吃午饭了吗？”“好的！”女孩们说。所以他们坐下来享用午餐。)
*   Lingling took out the sandwiches and Coke from the picnic basket. (玲玲从野餐篮里拿出了三明治和可乐。)
*   "I have some cookies. My mother made them yesterday," said Anne. (“我有一些饼干。我妈妈昨天做的，”安妮说。)
*   Peter gave them an apple each. "These sweet apples are from my garden," Peter said. (彼得给了他们每人一个苹果。“这些甜苹果来自我的花园，”彼得说。)
*   "This is a nice picnic!" said Dongdong. (“这是一次很棒的野餐！”冬冬说。)

### **Unit 7 What can I do?**
*   The children want to help old people. (孩子们想帮助老人。)
*   Jane says, "I can make tea or coffee for them." (简说：“我可以为他们泡茶或煮咖啡。”)
*   Lingling says, "I can ask Mum to make cakes for them." (玲玲说：“我可以让妈妈为他们做蛋糕。”)
*   Anne says, "I can sing to them and make them happy." (安妮说：“我可以给他们唱歌，让他们开心。”)
*   Mingming says, "I can help them on the road. They're weak and slow." (明明说：“我可以在路上帮助他们。他们身体虚弱而且走得慢。”)
*   Peter asks himself, "What can I do?" "I can clean their houses." (彼得问自己：“我能做什么？”“我可以打扫他们的房子。”)
*   Ms Li is a volunteer. She likes to help old people. (李女士是一名志愿者。她喜欢帮助老人。)
*   She takes them to the park near their homes and talks to them. She makes them happy. (她带他们去他们家附近的公园并和他们聊天。她让他们感到开心。)
*   She tells them interesting stories. They talk to one another and they enjoy themselves. (她给他们讲有趣的故事。他们互相交谈，玩得很开心。)
*   Ms Li feels happy to see this. She says, "I like to do this for them." (看到这些，李女士感到很高兴。她说：“我喜欢为他们做这些事。”)

### **Unit 8 We shouldn't waste water**
*   What are you doing, dear? I'm washing my blouse. (亲爱的，你在做什么？我在洗我的衬衫。)
*   We shouldn't waste water. Why? (我们不应该浪费水。为什么？)
*   There isn't enough water on the earth. You need water. Everyone needs water. (地球上没有足够的水。你需要水。每个人都需要水。)
*   I see. We must save every drop of water. I'm sorry, Mum. (我明白了。我们必须节约每一滴水。对不起，妈妈。)
*   Mr Zhou's office is not far away from his home, but he drives to work every day. (周先生的办公室离他家不远，但他每天都开车去上班。)
*   He likes his car very much, so he often washes his car and he uses a lot of water to do it. (他非常喜欢他的车，所以他经常洗车，而且用了很多水来洗。)
*   He tells Mrs Zhou, "I must keep my car very clean." (他告诉周太太：“我必须让我的车保持非常干净。”)
*   Mrs Zhou says to Mr Zhou, "You shouldn't wash your car too often. You shouldn't waste so much water." (周太太对周先生说：“你不应该太频繁地洗车。你不应该浪费这么多水。”)
*   Mrs Zhou goes shopping every day. She goes to the market on foot. (周太太每天都去购物。她步行去市场。)
*   She enjoys walking. She thinks it's good exercise. (她喜欢走路。她认为这是很好的锻炼。)

### **Unit 9 This bird is bigger than the first one**
*   Welcome to our clay art show. I'm Peter. I made a few birds with clay. Please take a look. Sure. (欢迎来到我们的泥塑艺术展。我是彼得。我用黏土做了几只鸟。请看一看。好的。)
*   This is the first one. It's a small bird. This is the second one. This bird is bigger than the first one. (这是第一只。它是一只小鸟。这是第二只。这只鸟比第一只大。)
*   Look at the third bird! It's bigger than the second one. (看第三只鸟！它比第二只大。)
*   This is the fourth one. It's bigger than the third one. Look at the fifth bird. It's the biggest. Wow! They are lovely. (这是第四只。它比第三只大。看第五只鸟。它是最大的。哇！它们很可爱。)
*   Once there were three big birds in a forest. They were Bob, Baker and Ben. (从前在森林里有三只大鸟。它们是鲍勃、贝克和本。)
*   Baker was bigger than Bob, but smaller than Ben. (贝克比鲍勃大，但比本小。)
*   Bob's singing was loud, but Baker's singing was louder. (鲍勃的歌声很响亮，但贝克的歌声更响亮。)
*   There were three small birds in the forest. They were Jane, Judy and Julia. (森林里有三只小鸟。它们是简、朱迪和朱莉娅。)
*   Judy was smaller than Julia, and Jane was the smallest. (朱迪比朱莉娅小，简是最小的。)
*   One day, the birds decided to sing together. A lot of people listened to them and said, "They are really the best singers in the forest!" (一天，鸟儿们决定一起唱歌。很多人听了它们唱歌，说：“它们真的是森林里最好的歌手！”)

### **Unit 10 I don't feel well today**
*   What's wrong with you? I don't feel well. I feel cold and I have a cough. (你怎么了？我感觉不舒服。我感觉冷，而且咳嗽。)
*   I have a headache. Let me check. You have a fever. You have a bad cold. (我头痛。让我检查一下。你发烧了。你得了重感冒。)
*   Drink more water. Take some medicine and rest in bed. (多喝水。吃点药，卧床休息。)
*   You can't go to school. Just have a good rest. Thank you, doctor. (你不能去上学了。就好好休息吧。谢谢医生。)
*   Yesterday, Dino's friends invited him to a big party. They enjoyed themselves and Dino came back home very late. (昨天，迪诺的朋友们邀请他参加一个盛大的聚会。他们玩得很开心，迪诺很晚才回家。)
*   Later, he didn't feel well. He felt cold and had a headache. He coughed and coughed. (后来，他感觉不舒服。他觉得冷，头痛。他咳了又咳。)
*   This morning, he was very ill. Tim wanted to take him to the hospital. (今天早上，他病得很重。蒂姆想带他去医院。)
*   But Dino said, "No! I don't want to take medicine. Water is good for me. Water is my medicine. I'll drink a lot of water and I will be fine." (但是迪诺说：“不！我不想吃药。水对我好处。水就是我的药。我会喝很多水，我会好起来的。”)

### **Unit 11 Shall we go to the theatre?**
*   Hello! Is that Anne? Yes. This is Anne. (你好！是安妮吗？是的。我是安妮。)
*   Shall we go to the cinema today? Well, it's a good idea. Or shall we go to the theatre? (我们今天去电影院好吗？嗯，是个好主意。或者我们去剧院好吗？)
*   A new play is on at Star Theatre. The time is 2:30 p.m. It's about a FAT CAT! (星光剧院在上演一部新剧。时间是下午2:30。是关于一只胖猫的！)
*   A FAT CAT? Great! I know that play. Let's go and see the play. (一只胖猫？太棒了！我知道那部剧。我们去看剧吧。)
*   What time shall we meet? 2:00 p.m. at Star Theatre. All right. (我们几点见面？下午2:00在星光剧院。好的。)

### **Unit 12 It's Christmas again!**
*   It's Christmas again! Here's a present for you. (又到圣诞节了！这是给你的礼物。)
*   Thank you, Mum. I can't wait to open it. (谢谢你，妈妈。我等不及要打开它了。)
*   People send Christmas cards to their friends to wish them a merry Christmas. (人们给朋友寄圣诞贺卡，祝他们圣诞快乐。)
*   People buy a lot of presents for their relatives and friends. (人们为亲戚朋友买很多礼物。)
*   Peter's family celebrates Christmas. Before Christmas, Peter and his parents decorate the Christmas tree. (彼得的家人庆祝圣诞节。在圣诞节前，彼得和他的父母装饰圣诞树。)
*   They go shopping to buy presents for their relatives and friends. The shopping centres are beautiful. (他们去购物，为亲戚朋友买礼物。购物中心很漂亮。)
*   They enjoy listening to the Christmas songs at these shopping centres. (他们喜欢在这些购物中心听圣诞歌曲。)
*   On Christmas Day, their friends and relatives visit them. They greet each other with "Merry Christmas". (在圣诞节那天，他们的朋友和亲戚拜访他们。他们用“圣诞快乐”互相问候。)
*   They also give presents to each other. They enjoy a hearty dinner. They eat roast turkey, ham, pudding and fruit cakes. (他们也互相赠送礼物。他们享用一顿丰盛的晚餐。他们吃烤火鸡、火腿、布丁和水果蛋糕。)

《六年级上册》的英语核心语法和句型在五年级的基础上进一步深化，重点学习了**一般过去时的运用**、**频度副词**、**一般将来时（will的用法）**、**情态动词（should/shall）**以及**形容词比较级**等核心知识点。

以下是全书各单元的核心语法和句型梳理：

**一、 谈论假期经历（一般过去时）**
*   **核心语法**：复习和巩固一般过去时，使用特殊疑问句询问过去发生的事情，并熟练掌握动词过去式（如 learn-learnt, read-read, play-played 等）的表达。
*   **核心句型**：
    *   — What did you do during the holidays?（你在假期里做了什么？）
    *   — I learnt writing. / I read many books. / I played games.（我学习了写作。/ 我读了很多书。/ 我玩了游戏。）

**二、 描述日常作息习惯（频度副词与一般现在时）**
*   **核心语法**：学习使用频度副词 `always`（总是）、`often`（经常）、`sometimes`（有时）、`never`（从不）结合一般现在时的第三人称单数形式来描述他人的生活习惯。
*   **核心句型**：
    *   Katie always gets up early.（凯蒂总是起得很早。）
    *   Katie often does her homework before dinner.（凯蒂经常在晚饭前做作业。）
    *   Katie sometimes plays chess with her father.（凯蒂有时和她爸爸下象棋。）

**三、 谈论事物的功能与用途（情态动词 can 表达能力）**
*   **核心语法**：使用情态动词 `can` 表达利用某物（如电脑）能够做些什么。
*   **核心句型**：
    *   We can email our friends / play games on the computer.（我们可以在电脑上给朋友发邮件/玩游戏。）
    *   We can search for a lot of things.（我们可以搜索很多东西。）

**四、 表达喜好与需求（enjoy doing 与 would like 的用法）**
*   **核心语法**：掌握 `enjoy + 动词ing` 形式表达喜欢做某事；复习使用 `would like`（想要）表达需求。
*   **核心句型**：
    *   I enjoy eating mooncakes / looking at the moon.（我喜欢吃月饼/赏月。）
    *   I'd like a box of lotus seed mooncakes and a box of red bean mooncakes.（我想要一盒莲蓉月饼和一盒红豆月饼。）

**五、 谈论天气预报（一般将来时 will be / there will be）**
*   **核心语法**：学习使用一般将来时 `will be` 和 `There will be` 来预测未来的天气情况。
*   **核心句型**：
    *   It will be sunny and warm tomorrow/next Monday.（明天/下周一将是晴朗温暖的。）
    *   There will be a light rain / a strong wind / a light snow.（将会有一场小雨/大风/小雪。）

**六、 谈论出行准备（一般将来时 will 与量词搭配）**
*   **核心语法**：使用 `I will...` 表达自己将要带来的物品，并重点学习不可数名词的量词搭配（如 a bottle of, a box of, a few bars of）。
*   **核心句型**：
    *   I will bring a bottle of orange juice.（我会带一瓶橙汁。）
    *   I will bring a few cans of Coke / a box of cakes / a few bars of chocolate.（我会带几罐可乐/一盒蛋糕/几块巧克力。）

**七、 提出帮助（What can I do...? 句型）**
*   **核心语法**：使用特殊疑问句 `What can I do for...?` 询问自己能为他人做什么，并用 `I can...` 提出具体的帮助。
*   **核心句型**：
    *   — What can I do for old people?（我能为老人们做什么？）
    *   — I can make tea for them / clean their houses / make them happy.（我可以为他们泡茶/打扫他们的房子/让他们开心。）

**八、 表达建议与社会规则（情态动词 should / shouldn't）**
*   **核心语法**：使用情态动词 `should`（应该）和 `shouldn't`（不应该）来表达应当遵守的环保规则或建议。
*   **核心句型**：
    *   We should plant more trees / keep the rivers clean.（我们应该种植更多的树/保持河流清洁。）
    *   We shouldn't waste water / litter / kill wild animals.（我们不应该浪费水/乱扔垃圾/捕杀野生动物。）

**九、 比较事物的特征（形容词比较级）**
*   **核心语法**：引入形容词比较级（如 bigger, smaller, taller, shorter, longer），并使用 `A is + 比较级 + than + B` 句型比较两个事物或人。
*   **核心句型**：
    *   This bird is bigger/smaller than the first one.（这只鸟比第一只大/小。）
    *   Tom is shorter than Mike.（汤姆比迈克矮。）
    *   Tom's hair is longer than Mike's.（汤姆的头发比迈克的长。）

**十、 谈论健康状况与疾病（have a... 句式）**
*   **核心语法**：使用 `What's wrong with you?` 询问对方身体状况，并使用 `I have a...` 表达自己患有的疾病或不适。
*   **核心句型**：
    *   — What's wrong with you?（你怎么了？）
    *   — I don't feel well. I feel cold and I have a cough / a fever / a headache.（我感觉不舒服。我感觉冷，而且咳嗽/发烧/头痛。）

**十一、 提出活动建议（Shall we...? 与 Let's... 句型）**
*   **核心语法**：使用 `Shall we...?`（我们……好吗？）礼貌地征求意见或提出建议，并用 `Let's...` 做出回应。
*   **核心句型**：
    *   — Shall we go to the cinema/theatre/concert hall?（我们去电影院/剧院/音乐厅好吗？）
    *   — Let's see a film / see a play / enjoy the music.（我们去看电影/看戏/欣赏音乐吧。）

**十二、 描述节日风俗（一般现在时描述客观事实）**
*   **核心语法**：运用一般现在时描述西方传统节日（圣诞节）的经典活动和习俗。
*   **核心句型**：
    *   Anne's family celebrates Christmas.（安妮的家人庆祝圣诞节。）
    *   Anne and her mother decorate their Christmas tree.（安妮和她的妈妈装饰她们的圣诞树。）
    *   Santa Claus gives out presents to children.（圣诞老人给孩子们分发礼物。）
